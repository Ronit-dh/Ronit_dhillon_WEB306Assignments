Justin Bieber

Biography

  • Justin Drew Bieber, born on March 1, 1994, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and actor. He was discovered through his YouTube covers and gained immense popularity at a young age.

    Biographical information sourced from Wikipedia

  • Justin Bieber's music career took off with hits like "Baby" and "Purpose." He has won numerous awards and has a massive fan following worldwide.

    Information sourced from Wikipedia

  • Justin Bieber has achieved great success in the music industry, with multiple Grammy Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and chart-topping albums.

    Information sourced from Wikipedia

Albums

  • "My World 2.0" is Justin Bieber's debut studio album, released in 2010. It includes hit singles like "Baby" and "Somebody to Love."

    Information sourced from Wikipedia

  • "Purpose" is Justin Bieber's fourth studio album, released in 2015. It features songs like "Sorry" and "Love Yourself."

    Information sourced from Wikipedia

  • "Changes" is Justin Bieber's fifth studio album, released in 2020. It showcases his evolving style and includes tracks like "Intentions" and "Yummy."
